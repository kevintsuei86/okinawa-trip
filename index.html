Okinawa Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

Arrive in Okinawa. Check into the hotel and relax.

Day 2: Explore Naha

  • Visit Shurijo Castle
  • Explore Kokusai Street
  • Try local food such as Okinawa soba

Day 3: Beach Day

Spend the day at the beautiful beaches of Okinawa, enjoy swimming and snorkeling.

Day 4: Island Hopping

Take a ferry to nearby islands like Tokashiki or Zamami.

Day 5: Departure

Last-minute shopping before heading to the airport.
